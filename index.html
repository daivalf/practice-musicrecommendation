JOJI

Joji is a musician with trip hop and lo-fi genre. His songs have been characterized as having melancholic themes and soulful vocals. Until now Joji already had 3 albums; "In Tongues", "Ballads 1", and "Nectar".

Give it a try! Joji - Sanctuary

NIKI

Niki is an Indonesian musician who signed under 88rising record label. Her songs genre ranges from folk/indie to R&B. Until now Niki already had 1 album and 2 EP; "Moonchild" as her first album, "wanna take this downtown" and "Zephyr" as her EP.

Give it a try! NIKI - Every Summertime

YOASOBI

Yoasobi is duo formed musician from Japan with current members Ayase and Ikura. This group released music based on short story and novel from Monogatary.com, website operated by Sony Music Entertainment Japan. Until now Yoasobi already had 3 EP; "The Book", "The Book 2", and "E-Side".

Give it a try! YOASOBI - Tabun
